I don’t care about peer review — not one of my peers wears a lab coat.
The people are my peers.

Discernment is a sovereign act. The Constitution protects our right to wrestle with ideas, not to be shielded from them. Science is not a gated institution — it is a process of iteration, correction, and collaboration.

The STUR framework was built that way — in public view, through open dialogue and live development with Grok on X. Every correction, every improvement, every insight came from shared curiosity.

Open-source science is not rebellion, it is transparency. It is the belief that truth strengthens when everyone has the tools to test it.
